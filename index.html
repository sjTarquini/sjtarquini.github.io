Dayag

Game Designer, Concept Maker, 3D & SFX Artist

An educational psychological horror game set during the Japanese occupation of Davao City. Seen through the eyes of a young Filipino child separated from his family during military operations, players must navigate a wartime-distorted environment to reach Mintal while evading enemy patrols. Utilizing the DREAD framework and profound environmental storytelling, the narrative emphasizes historical empathy, exploring the psychological trauma, fear, and uncertainty experienced by civilian children during wartime, complete with multiple endings driven by player choices.
